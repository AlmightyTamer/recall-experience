Chapter 2 · The product

Recall closes the gap before crisis.

Recall is an AI-native cognitive care platform — not another pill reminder. It listens, scores, verifies, and de-escalates across two purpose-built experiences linked by one real-time care graph.

Edge AI

Offline-first edge AI running on Cloudflare — fast, private, resilient.

Apple Health

Sleep, heart rate, and activity flow straight from the watch on her wrist.

Comfort Mode

Recall Cascade™ — automatic de-escalation the moment stability slips.

Two experiences, one brain

Built for both sides of care.

Margaret gets dignity and voice. Susan gets signal and control. Same data, different lenses — always in sync.

Patient · Clara for Margaret

A warm voice companion.

Voice-first — no menus, no shame. Clara knows her meds, her people, and her photos.

  • ElevenLabs voice with on-device context
  • Memory Photo Recap when loneliness rises
  • Apple Health sleep & heart-rate awareness
  • Tap-to-verify, vision-assisted medications
Caregiver · Recall AI for Susan

A specialist care advisor.

Live ACSE, med-verification logs, Clara transcripts, and appointment prep — before she even asks.

  • Morning briefings with pending meds
  • Cognitive weather forecast for the day
  • Trigger Comfort Mode from anywhere
  • Adherence trends & insight analytics

Shared intelligence

One brain, two lenses.

Patient and supervisor share the same offline-first care graph. When Margaret verifies Tylenol, Susan sees it instantly.

ACSE Engine

Scores stability, live.

A continuous cognitive-stability score. Below 50, Recall acts on its own.

Vision verify

The right pill, on-device.

Camera-based medication verification that never leaves the phone.

Storm Radar

Predicts sundowning.

Forecasts cognitive storms — like sundowning risk — before they hit.

The Recall difference

Prevention, not paperwork.

Most tools track tasks. Recall tracks cognitive stability — then acts. ACSE below 50 triggers Comfort Mode. Clara detects loneliness and opens family photos. Susan gets the briefing before she even asks.
